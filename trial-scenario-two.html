Today you are the nurse on Maple ward caring for Mr Stark's patient, Mrs Francine Olay who is in bed 6. Mrs Olay is receiving Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) for a Category III pressure ulcer on her sacrum. She already had the pressure ulcer when she was admitted to hospital and overall, the wound looks like it is improving with some slough and odour present.

After obtaining appropriate consent, you take a photograph of Mrs Olay's pressure ulcer. She has a temperature of 38°C, but is otherwise well, with normal blood results and observations.

Mrs Olay says she doesn't need a pain killer for changing the NPWT dressing. After cleaning the wound, you apply NPWT unit number 12345 at 125mmHg on intermittent therapy. Over the last 24 hours, the cannister has drained 110mls of serous exudate. The date of next wound review is in three days.
